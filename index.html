RSA Algorithm Step-by-Step

Step 1: Key Generation
  1. Select two distinct prime numbers p and q.
  2. Compute n = p × q.
    3. n = p × q
  3. Compute Euler's totient: φ(n) = (p-1) × (q-1).
    4. φ(n) = (p-1) × (q-1)
  4. Select an integer e such that 1 < e < φ(n) and gcd(e, φ(n)) = 1.
  5. Compute d, the modular inverse:
    d × e ≡ 1 (mod φ(n)).
    6. d × e ≡ 1 (mod φ(n))
  6. Public key: KU = {e, n}
  7. Private key: KR = {d, n}
Step 2: Encryption
  1. Take plaintext M where M < n.
  2. Compute ciphertext:
    3. C = Me mod n
Step 3: Decryption
  1. Take ciphertext C.
  2. Compute original message:
    3. M = Cd mod n
